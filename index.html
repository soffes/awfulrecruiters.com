I'm starting to compile a list of domains of recruiters. The goal is to get less email that I would never read anyway.

Disclaimer: I am definitely not saying these companies are awful. Simply that they are a source of undesirable email. This site is simply a list of domains. No claims are being made about the owners or their intentions.

Gmail Filter

Here is the search I use for my Gmail filter. I have my filter label them recruiter, never mark as important, and archive it. Gmail has a maximum search length so they are separated by new lines. (Scroll left and right. They're long.)

{% assign recruiters = site.data.recruiters.thirdparty | concat: site.data.recruiters.other %} {%comment%} The following loop is tricky and needs to be written without any whitespace because we are inside a pre tag where this matters. We calculate the modulo, store it in a variable and show a suffix or newline depending on the result {%endcomment%} 
{% for domain in recruiters | sort %}{% capture m %}{{forloop.index|modulo:40}}{% endcapture %}*@{{domain}}{% if m == '0' %}{{newline}}{% else %} OR {% endif %}{% endfor %}
